# Past Events

### Cryptographic Frontier 2021, Zagreb
<b>Open Problems in Ethereum Research at Eurocrypt 2021:</b>  This workshop brought the most interesting and challenging open cryptographic questions that Ethereum faces to the attention of academia.  We covered a large spectrum of research topics, such as multisignatures, commitments, verifiable delay functions, secure computation, zk-friendly hash functions and more.  <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/cryptofrontier21">Event webpage.</a>
